Best Of Assassin’s Creed

Assassin’s Creed 2 is a phenomenal sequel that expands the franchise’s parkour, combat, and stealth mechanics while delivering a captivating Renaissance-era story, following Ezio Auditore’s journey of revenge, growth, and brotherhood..
